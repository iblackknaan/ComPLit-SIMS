Sure, here are the reformatted modules considering multi-user login and authentication:

1. **User Authentication Module**:
   - **Objective**: Implement a secure login system to authenticate users and grant access based on their user type (admin, teacher, student, parent).
   - **Features**:
     - Login form with username and password fields.
     - Authentication mechanism to verify user credentials.
     - Session management to keep users logged in across pages.
     - Role-based access control to restrict access to specific functionalities based on user type.

2. **Admin Management Module**:
   - **Objective**: Provide functionalities for managing admin accounts and permissions.
   - **Features**:
     - Admin dashboard for managing other users, school settings, and system configurations.
     - CRUD operations for admin accounts (create, read, update, delete).
     - Permission management to assign different levels of access to admins.

3. **Teacher Management Module**:
   - **Objective**: Allow administrators to manage teacher accounts and related information.
   - **Features**:
     - Dashboard for adding, editing, and deleting teacher accounts.
     - Profile management for teachers to update their personal information.
     - Assignment of subjects and classes to teachers.

4. **Student Management Module**:
   - **Objective**: Enable administrators and teachers to manage student records, attendance, and academic performance.
   - **Features**:
     - Student registration form for capturing student details.
     - Attendance tracking system to record student attendance.
     - Generation of student report cards to monitor academic progress.

5. **Parent Portal Module**:
   - **Objective**: Provide a platform for parents to access information about their children's education.
   - **Features**:
     - Parent login portal with access to their child's academic records, attendance, and school announcements.
     - Communication tools for parents to interact with teachers and school administrators.

6. **Class Management Module**:
   - **Objective**: Facilitate the organization and management of classes, including scheduling and assignment of teachers.
   - **Features**:
     - Class creation and assignment of teachers.
     - Timetable generation for class schedules.
     - Management of classroom resources and facilities.

7. **Communication Module**:
   - **Objective**: Establish effective communication channels between administrators, teachers, students, and parents.
   - **Features**:
     - Messaging system for sending announcements, reminders, and notifications.
     - Forums or discussion boards for collaborative learning and discussions.
     - Email integration for sending automated notifications to stakeholders.

8. **Security Module**:
   - **Objective**: Ensure the security and integrity of the school management system and user data.
   - **Features**:
     - Implementation of secure login mechanisms (e.g., encryption, hashing) to protect user credentials.
     - Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
     - Role-based access control to enforce user permissions and prevent unauthorized access.

9. **Reporting and Analytics Module**:
   - **Objective**: Provide administrators and teachers with tools for generating reports and analyzing data.
   - **Features**:
     - Predefined report templates for student attendance, academic performance, and other metrics.
     - Customizable dashboards for visualizing data and tracking key performance indicators.
     - Data export functionality for sharing reports with stakeholders.

10. **Integration Module**:
    - **Objective**: Enable seamless integration with other school systems and third-party applications.
    - **Features**:
      - API integration to connect with external platforms (e.g., learning management systems, accounting software).
      - Single sign-on (SSO) functionality for accessing multiple systems with a single set of credentials.
      - Data synchronization mechanisms to ensure consistency across integrated systems.

These modules should form the foundation of your school management system, allowing for comprehensive management of various aspects of the educational institution while ensuring secure access and efficient communication among stakeholders.
